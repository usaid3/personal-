4. 📉 **Sales loss** — Customer order karne mein time leta hai
5. 📱 **Modern look nahi** — Competitors digital ja rahe hain

**Aap in 5 problems ka solution ho! Aap HERO ho!** 💪

## 💰 PART 4: PRICING STRATEGY

### 📊 Pakistan Market ke liye Pricing:

#### 🎯 **TIER 1: Basic Package** — Rs. 8,000 - 12,000 (One-time)
- Single design (jo aapne already bana liya)
- Up to 30 menu items
- WhatsApp ordering
- 1 month free support
- **Best for:** Small cafes, dhabas, takeaway

#### 🎯 **TIER 2: Standard Package** — Rs. 15,000 - 25,000 (One-time)
- Premium design (any from 7)
- Unlimited menu items
- WhatsApp + Cart + Map
- QR code design (printed)
- 3 months free support
- **Best for:** Medium restaurants

#### 🎯 **TIER 3: Premium Package** — Rs. 35,000 - 50,000 (One-time)
- Custom design (choice of 7)
- Hosting included (1 year)
- Custom domain (yourrestaurant.com)
- 6 months free updates
- Training session
- **Best for:** Big restaurants, chains

#### 💡 **MONTHLY MODEL (Recurring Income!):**
- Rs. 1,500 - 3,000 / month
- Includes hosting + updates + support
- **Yeh BEST hai** — har month income!
